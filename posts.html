Nature #1

The Immortal Jellyfish 🪼

The *Turritopsis dohrnii* can technically live forever. It can transform its cells back to their earliest form and start its life cycle all over again.

Space #2

Diamond Rain ♦

On Saturn and Jupiter, it literally rains diamonds. The intense pressure turns carbon into crystals before they fall to the planet's core.

Animals #3

Cow Friendships 🐮

Cows have "best friends" and they get stressed out when they are separated from one another.

History #4

The Shortest War ⚔

The Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896 is the shortest war in history, lasting only 38 minutes before a surrender was called.

Science #5

Human Atoms 👨⚛

If you removed all the "empty space" from the atoms that make up every human on Earth, the entire population would fit inside a single sugar cube.

Animals #6

Platypus Glow 🦫

Under a UV light, a platypus glows a blue-green color. Scientists still aren't entirely sure why this happens!

Geography #7

Moving Mountains ⛰

Mount Everest shrinks and grows. Due to tectonic activity, it moves about 4cm northeast every single year.

Food #8

Strawberry Lies 🍓

Strawberries are not actually berries, but bananas, pumpkins, and watermelons technically are!

Human Body #9

Unique Tongues 👅

Just like fingerprints, every single person has a completely unique tongue print.

Technology #10

The First Bug 🐛

The term "computer bug" was coined when a literal moth got stuck inside a computer at Harvard in 1947.
